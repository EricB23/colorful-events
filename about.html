About The Midnight Club

The Midnight Club on Bell Road was inspired by the early 2000's racing game created by Rockstar Games and brings a new vibe and level of entertainment in the South Nashville Area. We keep it clean when it comes to the streets: No cheating, No ILLEGAL activity, and even better street racing fully supervised.

If you have any questions or would like to be apart of this exclusive club, feel free to reach out to our email at MidnightClubSN@Bellroad.com
